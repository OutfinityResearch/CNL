# DoCO (Document Components Ontology)

**Source:** http://purl.org/spar/doco
**Format:** Turtle
**License:** Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) (SPAR Ontologies standard)

## Purpose
Describes the structural components of a document (e.g., Chapter, Section, Paragraph, Footnote).
Extension of FaBiO and FRBR.

## Key Concepts
- `Chapter`, `Section`, `Paragraph`
- `Preface`, `Appendix`, `Bibliography`
- `Figure`, `Table`

## Usage in CNL
Used in `literature.cnl` to analyze the structure and quality of documents.
