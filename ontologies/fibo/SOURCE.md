# FIBO (Financial Industry Business Ontology)

**Source:** Custom Mini-Ontology (based on EDM Council FIBO)
**Format:** Turtle
**License:** MIT (for this mini version)

## Purpose
Core financial and business concepts.

## Key Concepts
- `Agent` (Person, Organization)
- `Contract`
- `Currency`
- `Corporation`

## Usage in CNL
Used in `legal.cnl` for commercial contracts and business entities.
